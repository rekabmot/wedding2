The Venue

The North East of England has dozens of beautiful wedding destinations.

However, located within the countryside of County Durham, Beamish Hall Hotel is definitely among the most special.

The hotel, which is a stones throw away from Beamish Open Air Museem is a converted 13th century manor house. The wedding ceremony will take place in the Shafto Hall, with the wedding breakfast and reception following afterwards in the Winter Gardens.

Tom and Sarah have booked exclusive use of the hotel for the day of the wedding, meaning that the only people on site will be their families and friends. If you've been invited to stay at the hotel on the night of the wedding, you should have had information from the bride or groom. If not, please get in touch ASAP!
